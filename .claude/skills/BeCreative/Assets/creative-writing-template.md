## Tips for Creative Writing

1. **Be specific about constraints:** "avoiding clichés about X" helps push creativity
2. **Specify unusual perspectives:** "from the viewpoint of..." adds uniqueness
3. **Request specific literary devices:** "using synesthesia" or "with unreliable narrator"
4. **Set creative challenges:** "without using the letter 'e'" or "in reverse chronology"
5. **Combine genres:** "noir detective story set in fantasy world"
6. **Use deep thinking:** The recommended template leverages deep thinking for better results
7. **Challenge assumptions:** Ask for approaches that go beyond the first obvious ideas
